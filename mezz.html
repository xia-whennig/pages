MEZZ boards

The MEZZ boards are small, flexible adapter boards for analog or digital signals. They include

  • MEZZ01: 8x SMB to 0.1in header
  • MEZZ02: 8x SMB to 0.1in header or FCS8 microcoax
  • MEZZ03: 8x SMB to 0.1in header
  • MEZZ04: GRF1 ganged RF to 0.1in header

MEZZ01

MEZZ01 comes in 4 standard configurations

  • LBSJ: 8x LEMO coax input/output buffered to/from 34x 0.1in header, direction jumper selectable
    Intended for exernal digital I/O signals connecting to headers on the Pixie-16 x1/x2 or MZTIO boards
  • SBJS: 8x SMB coax input/output buffered to/from 34x 0.1in header, direction jumper selectable
    Intended for exernal digital I/O signals connecting to headers on the Pixie-16 x1/x2 or MZTIO boards
  • SCER: 8x SMB coax to/from 34x 0.1in header (direct copper to even pins), stackable with SCOS
    Intended for analog signals
  • SCOS: 8x SMB coax to/from 34x 0.1in header (direct copper to odd pins), stackable with SCER
    Intended for analog signals

Applications

  • Trigger I/O for Pixie-16 x1/x2 and MZTIO
  • Conversion of analog coax signals to 0.1in flat cables or vice versa
  • SCER and SCOS can stack to convert 16x SMB to 16x signals in a 34x flat cable
  • 5 SCER/SCOS stacks can be mounted on a 19in panel

MEZZ02

MEZZ02 comes in 3 standard configurations

  • 8x SMB coax to/from 34x 0.1in header (direct copper to even or odd pins), stackable
    Intended for analog signals
  • 34x 0.1 header to/from FCS8 microcoax connector (direct copper to even or odd pins), stackable
    Intended for analog signals
  • 8x SMB coax to/from FCS8 microcoax connector (direct copper)
    Intended for analog signals

Applications

  • Conversion of analog coax signals to 0.1in flat cables or FCF8 cables
  • Signal adapter board for some Pixie-16 x2 or Pixie-Net XL variants
  • Equivalent to MEZZ01 but with additional FCS8 connector

MEZZ03

MEZZ03 comes in 1 standard configuration

  • 8x SMB (vertical) coax input/output to/from 16x 0.1in header, direct copper
    Intended for analog signals

Applications

  • Conversion of analog coax signals to 0.1in flat cables
  • Signal adapter board for some Pixie-16 x2 or Pixie-Net XL variants
  • Can be mounted on Pixie-Net XL front panel
  • Similar to MEZZ01 but no buffer, smaller flat cable
